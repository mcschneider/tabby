Simple Toggle Tabs

Tabby is a set of simple, unstyled toggle tabs powered by vanilla JS.

Superheros
Spiderman, Batman, or Iron Man... which one is your favorite?

Ice Cream
Chocolate, vanilla or strawberry?

Seasons
Winter, summer, spring or fall?

Built by Chris Ferdinandi. Free to use under the MIT License.

Tabby is an add-on for Kraken, a lightweight, mobile-first boilerplate for front-end web developers. It works great as a standalone product, too.
